About Bipolar I Disorder

Manic Episodes

Characterized by elevated mood, increased energy, racing thoughts, and impulsive behavior. Can include feelings of euphoria, grandiosity, and decreased need for sleep.

Depressive Episodes

Marked by persistent sadness, loss of interest, fatigue, difficulty concentrating, and feelings of worthlessness. Can significantly impact daily functioning.

Mixed Episodes

Characterized by experiencing symptoms of both mania and depression simultaneously. Can include agitation, racing thoughts, and suicidal ideation alongside high energy levels.

About This Artwork

This interactive piece visualizes the dramatic shifts between emotional states. The contrasting colors, speeds, and intensities reflect the lived experience of Bipolar I Disorder. Use the controls to explore different states and view your mood timeline.

Note: This is an artistic representation, not a diagnostic tool. If you or someone you know is experiencing symptoms, please consult a healthcare professional.
